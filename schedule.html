Our schedule is not set in stone, though you should generally be available weekends (Friday and Satruday nights) from about 8:00 - 12:30 EST. The guild is centered out of the approximate New England area, so we have a tendency to reference times in EST. We apologize to anyone in other time zones for which this may be confusing.

The guild will aim to complete multiple (2 - 4) Karazhan lockouts each week, and more of those times will be anounced around the schedules of incoming members. One of these groups will most likely be a weekday.

Tentative T5 Schedule:
Karazhan: Tue(8:00-10:30)?, Fri(3:30-6:00)*, Sat(2:30-5:00)
Serpentshrine Cavern: Fri(8:00-12:30)*
The Eye: Sat(8:00-12:00)*
Magtheridon: Sun(8:00-8:30)
Gruul: Sat(8:30-9:30)

Raids marked with an '*' are fixed-date raids. These times will only be adjusted by up to an hour if needed.
