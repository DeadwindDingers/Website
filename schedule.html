Our schedule is not set in stone, though you should generally be available weekends (Friday and Satruday nights) from about 7:00 - 11:30 CT. The guild is centered out of the approximate New England area, so we have a tendency to reference times in EST by mistake. We apologize to anyone in other time zones for which this may be confusing.

The guild will aim to complete multiple (2 - 4) Karazhan lockouts each week, and more of those times will be anounced around the schedules of incoming members. At least one of these groups will most likely be a weekday.
