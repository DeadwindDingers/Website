  Our schedule will be somewhat dependent on the current content level we are in and how many raids we are juggling for each lockout period, though the core raid times will be Friday and Sunday nights starting around 8:00EST/7:00 Realm Time. These times are tentative, although progression raids will always be scheduled between Friday and Sunday. We will atempt to maintain a Sunday night close time before 12:00AM EST. Note that the guild's leadership is (mostly) in EST and will naturally refer to times in this timezone, so be warned!

The guild will aim to complete multiple (2 - 4) Karazhan lockouts each week, and more of those times will be anounced around the schedules of incoming members. One of these groups will most likely be a weekday.
