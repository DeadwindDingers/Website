Our schedule is not set in stone, though you should generally be available weekends (Friday and Satruday nights) from about 7:00 - 11:30 CT (should be shorter but better safe than sorry!). The guild is centered out of the approximate New England area, so we have a tendency to accidentally reference times in EST by mistake. We apologize to anyone in other time zones for which this may be confusing.

Please note that durations on larger raids are largely speculative, and that we will adjust the schedule as needed in accordance to what the guild and its members need. Below is an approximate look at the future scheduling. Note that each tier has alternating weeks "1" and "2" in case certain players can only attend one of the two main raid nights, meaning you can still do each raid if you can only raid on Friday or Saturday. Sunday is reserved for "catch-up", so don't feel bad if you cannot raid Sundays or have to leave early due to work the following day. We may move the catch-up day if a different day alligns better with the schedules of our members and will try to keep times as early as possible. The current placement of Karazhan (2) on Tuesday is an arbitrary weekday that will likely change.
