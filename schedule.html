Our schedule is not set in stone, though you should generally be available weekends (Friday and Satruday nights) from about 7:00 - 10:30 EST (should be shorter but better safe than sorry!). The guild is centered out of the approximate New England area, so times will be given in EST. Note that this is not the same as server time on our home server Korgath-US. Please note that durations on larger raids are largely speculative, and that we will adjust the schedule as needed in accordance to what the guild and its members need.

Our 90 progression nights are Friday and Saturday. Those joining the 90 Project should be available on these nights of the week during most weeks.

Our completed content brackets (60 and 70) will have raid events on some Sundays. Post in Discord if you would like to see a particular raid scheduled.
